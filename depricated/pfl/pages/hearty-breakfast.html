‘Shots, Hits and Grace’: a discussion with William Wade

What?

Join us in the relaxing atmosphere of Crema³ café to hear the remarkable story of one Northern Irish troublemaker who went from a life of destruction to a life of hope in Christ. William Wade, a former army boxing champion and soldier in Colchester Garrison, recounts his journey to faith, revealing the difference that Christianity has made in his life. Cake, freshly ground coffee and alcoholic drinks will be available for purchase. We hope to see you there.

Where?

Crema³ Coffee, Colchester

14-16, Long Wyre Street, Colchester, Essex, CO1 1LH

When?

Friday 25th March, 8:00pm to 9:30pm
