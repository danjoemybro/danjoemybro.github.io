Star Runner

Star Runner is a 2D side scrolling game where you play as a Space Ship that goes on an adventure, trying to gather coins and fighting monsters out among the asteroids whilst making sure you have enough fuel to get home. The game uses a combination of Box2D physics and resolution scaling so that the game runs on PC, tablet and mobile.

Open Source Development

flutter_series

Flutter Series is a flutter plugin for improved row and column widgets with added spacing and optional interleaved dividers. This functionality is loosely based on Swift UI's HStack and VStack spacing attribute. This plugin has helped create readable code, reducing padding and margin headaches of widgets in rows and columns and has reduced unnecessary nesting.

Production

One of my main interests throughout my life has been music production. I have spent many years working on creating well produced music in my own time and at university. I enjoy creating a mixture of acoustic and electronic music, usually inspired by the progressive rock genre.

Logic Pro

I have an extensive knowledge of using Logic Pro, which has been my main DAW for many years. Within it, I composed music for videos and video games, created complex sampler instruments and produce songs to release on Spotify.

Captain King

One of my largest creative endevours is the composing, recording and releasing my EP/album Captain King as my final year project at university. The idea was to create an EP with 24 songs totalling an average length of one minute to highlight how modern streaming services are effecting the music industry as a whole. With the advent artists being payed per stream rather than playtime songs are getting shorter.
